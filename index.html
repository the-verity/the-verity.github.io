Characteristics

  • Thrillers are known for their ability to keep readers on the edge of their seats.
  • Thrillers typically feature intricate and multifaceted plots with unexpected twists and turns.
  • Readers root for the protagonist as they face danger and obstacles.
  • A well-defined antagonist or antagonist force is a key element in thrillers.
  • The urgency of the situation adds to the tension.
  • This can enhance suspense and reveal hidden layers of the story.
  • This adds complexity to the mystery.
